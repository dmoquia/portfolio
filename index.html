About

Hello, my name is Dennis I'm a BSIT graduate and former senior process executive at Infosys with more than 10 years of experience in customer service like providing back office support to a client as well as experienced with various line of business including telecom, finance account, and and providing B2B admin support to as US employee counterpart. I am excited to be making a career shift from a BPO employee to a frontend developer. While working in the BPO industry, I gained valuable skills in communication, problem-solving, and working in a team environment. I also developed a strong interest in technology and web development, which led me to pursue a career in frontend development. I have been actively learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as working on personal projects to gain practical experience. I am confident that my skills and experiences will make me a strong asset as a frontend developer, and I am eager to continue learning and growing in this field.
